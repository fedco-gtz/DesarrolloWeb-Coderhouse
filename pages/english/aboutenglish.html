ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

Since our inception in 2021, we have worked hard to rescue, rehabilitate and find forever homes for thousands of pets. Thanks to the support of our volunteers, adopters and sponsors, we have managed to change the lives of countless animals.

OUR MISSION

We are an organization committed to the welfare of animals and the promotion of adoption responsible. Our mission is to provide a loving and safe home for pets in need, and promote awareness about animal care and protection.

WORK TEAM

Our team is made up of passionate animal lovers, including veterinarians, caregivers, trainers and professionals dedicated to ensuring the well-being and happiness of each pet under our care.
