PRIVACY POLICY

Happy Pets USA (hereinafter, “Happy Pets”) is pleased to communicate this privacy policy (hereinafter, the “Policy”) of its web pages (“Website”). Feel confident that Happy Pets protects the privacy of the data of those who access our Website.

Use of Cookies

We want to make our Website as useful and intuitive as possible, while respecting the privacy of our users. Happy Pets uses “cookies” (pieces of information that a page stores in a browser or device to save the user's visit records) only to obtain impersonal information with the aim of improving your online experience. For example, like most websites on the Internet, we keep track of the number of people who visit the page on an anonymous basis. Happy Pets does not associate the information in “cookies” with personal information. To configure or deactivate “cookies”, you can access the configuration panel corresponding to your web browser. Keep in mind that, if you block the installation of “cookies”, you may not be able to access all or part of the Website or some of the functionalities of the Happy Pets digital tools.

Type of information we collect and how it is used

Happy Pets only collects identifying information only when the data is voluntarily provided by people who visit the Website. This information is used by Happy Pets, with the prior consent of its owner, to improve the page, respond to messages received, improve your experience and send promotional and commercial communications, all in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Law. No. 25,326.

The information is not sold or transferred to third parties.

The user of the Website gives their consent so that their personal data to which Happy Pets has access may be processed locally or through the companies of which they are a member or companies sponsoring the site for the purpose for which they were collected.
